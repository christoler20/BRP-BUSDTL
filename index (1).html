The Project

174 Years of Policy, Promise,
and Unequal Education

This documentary timeline traces the legal frameworks, housing policies, community movements, and district decisions that have shaped educational opportunity for Black and descendant students in Berkeley — from California's earliest acts of racialized exclusion through the BUSD Reparations Task Force of 2024.

Built by the UC Berkeley Public Policy Data Lab in partnership with the Mayor's Office of Innovation and the Black Reparations Project, the timeline connects present-day disparities to the documented historical choices that produced them.
